Bryan Wong

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Graduate School of Data Science (GSDS), KAIST, advised by Prof. Mun Yong Yi. I am also an incoming visiting Ph.D. researcher at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), A*STAR, under the supervision of Dr. Xun Xu.

I am grateful to be a recipient of the A*STAR Research Attachment Programme (ARAP), which supports my research for 14 months at I2R, A*STAR.

My recent research focuses on multimodal AI in the medical domain, especially VLMs, multimodal LLMs, and agentic AI for gigapixel medical images. I am also fortunate to collaborate closely with Dr. Huazhu Fu at IHPC, A*STAR.
