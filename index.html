About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Data Science at KAIST, advised by Prof. Mun Yong Yi. My current research focuses on vision-language models (VLMs) for medical image analysis, specifically in computational pathology.

During my Ph.D., I have been fortunate to collaborate with and be mentored by Dr. Huazhu Fu at A*STAR. In 2023, I received my M.S. in Data Science from KAIST, after completing a B.S. in Computer Science at NTUST in 2021, where I graduated one year early.
